A personal portfolio website that showcases professional skills and projects, developed with Vue.js and featuring responsive design for various devices.

- **State Management**: Vuex
- **Routing**: Vue Router
- **Styling**: SCSS
- **Build Tools**: Webpack
- **Deployment**: Netlify
